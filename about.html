BangsaenAI is a research laboratory dedicated to bridging the gap between theoretical mathematics and real-world engineering.

Our Mission

In an era where "AI" is often synonymous with Large Language Models, we take a different path. We focus on Physics-Informed Machine Learning. We believe that the best way to control a drone or stabilize a power grid isn't just to feed it data, but to teach it the laws of physics.

Founded in Bangsaen, Thailand, our lab operates at the intersection of Control Theory, Dynamical Systems, and Embedded Engineering.

Aerial Robotics

Developing non-linear flight controllers using Koopman Operator Theory to achieve stability in chaotic wind conditions.

Smart Energy Grids

Solving the "Zero-Inertia" problem in renewable energy grids using differential geometry and high-speed signal processing.
